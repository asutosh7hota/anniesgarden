Annies garden is a setup with a vision to give a new dimension to Balcony Garden,Terrace garden and Landscaping.It is also to plant a personal garden in a small urban space.

We are the earth lovers and this passion helps us to make balcony gardens, Terrace Gardens and Landscaping that woudld sooth not only the mind but the soul too. It not only adds a serence beauty to your interior but also purifies the air giving you a energetic start to your day everytime.
